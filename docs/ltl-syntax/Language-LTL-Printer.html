class ToText a where Source

printer of LTL formulae in spot's and spin's format

>>> toText $ (Binary U (AP "p") (Unary G (AP "q")))
"(p U (G q))"

>>> toText $ SpinFormula  (Binary U (AP "p") (Unary G (AP "q")))
"(p U ([] q))"
